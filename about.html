The Intercollegiate Business Convention (IBC) is the largest undergraduate conference in the world and the keystone event organized by Harvard Undergraduate Women in Business (HUWIB). IBC 2014 will be held on Saturday, October 18 at the Hynes Convention Center in Boston. This year, the objective of IBC is to build a global support network and community of empowerment for collegiate women interested in business.

Now in its tenth year, IBC attracts over 1,200 registrants and over 100 professionals from a diverse range of industries. Attendees will be enthralled by three inspiring keynote speakers, they will interact with peers and professionals over a delicious catered lunch, and they will attend an exclusive career fair and networking session.
