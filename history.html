This year, we celebrate the decennial anniversary of the Intercollegiate Business Convention. As a milestone for ten years of growth in both size and opportunity, this year’s IBC will continue to be a forum for driven young women to discuss relevant and emerging trends, gain both concrete skills and applicable advice, and join a network of peers and inspiring professionals who will guide and engage them throughout their careers. We will also recognize a keynote speaker who demonstrates a passion for making an impact through exceptional business leadership, who drives positive change within her company, and who inspires women to continue to succeed, with the IBC Decennial Award.
